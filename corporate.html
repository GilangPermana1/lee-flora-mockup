Frequently Asked Questions

Corporate Flower Questions

What is the "combo" approach to corporate flower installations?
Our combo approach blends preserved flowers with artificial greenery and blooms to create arrangements that look 100% natural while being 30–40% more cost-effective than using all-preserved flowers. For example, a reception wall might use preserved moss as the base with artificial orchids as accents.
How much do corporate flower installations cost in Singapore?
Costs vary based on size and complexity. A simple reception desk arrangement starts from $80–$150 using our combo approach. Lobby feature walls range from $800–$1,500. These are one-time costs lasting 2–5 years, compared to fresh subscriptions costing $200–$800/month.
Can preserved flowers survive in Singapore's air-conditioned offices?
Yes — preserved flowers are ideal for air-conditioned environments. The controlled humidity of most Singapore offices actually helps extend their lifespan, often beyond the standard 2–3 year estimate.
Does Lee Flora do installation, or just supply the flowers?
Lee Flora is a wholesale supplier — we supply materials at trade prices. For installation, we can connect you with professional florists in our directory who specialise in corporate and event setups.
What is the minimum order for corporate clients?
There is no strict minimum order. However, wholesale pricing tiers apply — the more you order, the better the per-unit cost. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your specific requirements and get a tailored quote.
