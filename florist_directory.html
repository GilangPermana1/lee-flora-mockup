Directory FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Florist Directory

How do I get listed in the Lee Flora Florists Directory?
To be listed, you need to be an active customer of Lee Flora Wholesale — purchasing your preserved, dried, or artificial flowers from us. Apply using the button above, and our team will review your application. Listing is free of charge.
Are these florists employed by Lee Flora?
No — the florists in our directory are independent businesses. Lee Flora Wholesale is a supplier; the florists are our customers and partners. We list them to help connect skilled florists with customers who are looking for their services.
Can I hire a florist from this directory for my wedding?
Yes — many of the florists in our directory specialise in weddings and wedding-related arrangements including bridal bouquets, hantaran, and table décor. Contact the individual florist directly for quotes and availability.
Do these florists offer delivery across Singapore?
Delivery policies vary by florist. Most offer island-wide delivery for completed arrangements, though terms and fees differ. Contact your chosen florist directly for their specific delivery details.
